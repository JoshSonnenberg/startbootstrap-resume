Chicago, IL | sonnenberg.business@gmail.com

I’ve always been enamored by the way it’s possible to create something from nothing as a software developer, and am even more so by mobile development. Creating something that is both visually and technologically stunning elicits a sense of excitement, achievement, and wonder that drives me to always best my previous work, and to help others best theirs.

My journey into mobile began as a student in October 2014 when I started working for the University of Wisconsin Milwaukee’s Mobile Innovation Lab, more fondly known as the UWM App Brewery. While working at the App Brewery I went from implementing solutions to issues with pre-existing apps, to leading student teams to develop new ones.

Continuing on my mobile journey, I've found myself working for Kin + Carta (formerly Solstice) as a Sr. Technical Consultant. Starting off as a Technical Analyst, then Technical Consultant, I worked my way through the ranks as I advanced from a dev position, to working as a Team Lead. Since becoming a Team Lead, I've lead four teams across two projects. In my most recent iOS project, I built the core architecture from the ground up using MVVM-C. I also had the responsibility and delight to train five devs, two new college grads, two devs new to iOS, and one Jr. iOS dev. In this position, I've worked on two major iOS Native apps, done rapid prototyping in React Native, and have tried my hand at Fullstack with .Net and Angular.
